m&m's candyman remix

To celebrate M&M’S 75th anniversary, we hired Aloe Blacc and Zedd to remake the classic “Candyman” song from the 70s. We then gave customers the chance to remix the new track through an AR-based web app, in which moving real M&M’S around in your phone’s camera view altered the track in real-time.

Awards: FWA Site of the Day, Bronze Kinsale Shark (x2), FAB Gold
Press: Adage
