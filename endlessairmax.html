foot locker x nike the endless world of air max

Sneakerheads use Google Drive and other shared docs to track drops and resell prices. So for Foot Locker and Nike, we made a shoe store entirely in Google Slides to promote the latest line of Air Maxes. The store resulted in a 43% increase in revenue for Foot Locker from the Air Max line of products.
